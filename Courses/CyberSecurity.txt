Course: Cybersecurity Fundamentals

Description:
This course covers the fundamentals of cybersecurity, including principles of information security, threat detection, and basic defensive strategies.

Topics Covered:
1. Introduction to Cybersecurity
   - Understanding the cybersecurity landscape
   - Key cybersecurity concepts and terminologies

2. Threats and Attack Vectors
   - Types of cyber threats
   - Common attack vectors

3. Defensive Strategies
   - Antivirus and anti-malware tools
   - Firewalls and intrusion detection/prevention systems

4. Cryptography Basics
   - Encryption and decryption
   - Secure communication

5. Project: Basic Security Audit
   - Conduct a basic security audit on a system

Recommended Resources:
- Cybersecurity documentation and guidelines
- Online courses on cybersecurity fundamentals
- Books on cybersecurity
